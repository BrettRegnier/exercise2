Life is a Mountain

By Jenny Sasseville - 2019

Getting closer to the top

You can almost grasp it

Take a moment, breathe, and step forward

Look down, see how far you've come

Your mountain is now beneath you

Life is a Mountain

--

Jenny Sasseville - 2019
